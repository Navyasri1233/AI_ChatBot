- 30-day return window from date of delivery

- Return shipping is FREE for defective or incorrect items
- Customer-initiated returns: $7.99 return shipping fee deducted from refund
- Refunds processed within 5-7 business days after receiving returned item

REPLACEMENT POLICY:
- Defective items can be replaced within 90 days of purchase
- No-cost replacement for manufacturer defects
- Replacement items ship within 2 business days of approval
- Extended replacement warranty available for purchase (up to 3 years)
- Advanced replacement option: New item ships before returning defective one

WARRANTY INFORMATION:
- All products include manufacturer's standard warranty
- Extended warranty plans available: 1-year ($29.99), 2-year ($49.99), 3-year ($79.99)
- Accidental damage protection available on select products
- Warranty claims processed through TechMart customer service

CANCELLATION POLICY:
- Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placement for full refund
- After 2 hours but before shipping: $5 processing fee applies
- Once shipped: Follow standard return policy
- Custom/personalized orders cannot be cancelled

PRICE MATCH GUARANTEE:
- We match prices from authorized retailers
- Valid for 14 days from purchase date
- Difference refunded as store credit
- Excludes clearance, refurbished, and marketplace items

================================================================================
                      FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: How do I track my order?
A: You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships. You can also 
   track your order by logging into your TechMart account and visiting the "My Orders" 
   section.

Q: Can I change my shipping address after placing an order?
A: Yes, but only if your order hasn't shipped yet. Contact customer service 
   immediately at 1-800-TECH-MART or support@techmartglobal.com.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, Apple Pay, 
   Google Pay, and TechMart gift cards. We do not accept personal checks or money orders.

Q: Do you offer price protection?
A: Yes! If an item you purchased goes on sale within 14 days of your purchase, 
   we'll refund the difference as store credit. Contact customer service with your 
   order number and the current sale price.

Q: How do I initiate a return?
A: Log into your account, go to "My Orders," select the order, and click "Return Items." 
   Print the prepaid return label (if applicable) and ship the item back in its 
   original packaging.

Q: Are there any items that cannot be returned?
A: Yes. Software (if opened), digital downloads, gift cards, personalized/custom items, 
   and items marked as "Final Sale" cannot be returned unless defective.

Q: Do you offer student or military discounts?
A: Yes! Students with valid .edu email addresses receive 10% off most items. 
   Military personnel and veterans receive 15% off with valid ID verification.

Q: What if my item arrives damaged?
A: Contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damage. We'll arrange 
   for a free replacement or full refund, including return shipping costs.

Q: Can I buy extended warranties after my purchase?
A: Extended warranties must be purchased at the time of checkout. They cannot be 
   added later.

Q: Do you have physical stores?
A: Currently, TechMart Global operates exclusively online. However, we have partner 
   service centers in major cities for warranty repairs and technical support.
